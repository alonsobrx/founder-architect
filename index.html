About This Work

This research is produced by Brasidas Strategies, an independent research operation founded by Arnoldo Alonso. All analysis is powered by Ayala™ — a proprietary neurointelligence research system developed over more than a decade. Publications are released free of charge for public use, distribution, and adaptation.

Mission

The goal is mass education. To give ordinary people the same analytical tools used by investors, lobbyists, and policy professionals — and to apply those tools in the public interest. Knowledge is the only antidote to extraction.

Independence & Disclosure

Brasidas Strategies has no financial interest in any subject covered by its publications, no client relationship with any party to any transaction analyzed, and no affiliation with any economic development organization, lobbying firm, or government agency. All research is conducted independently and released as open-source public interest material. No compensation is received for any publication.
